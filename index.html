Hi, I'm TJLlamaCraft aka Tia-Leoni Joseph, a South African Game Design student! My passions are art and design. I love thinking about how to make a game or piece of art an experience for the viewer. I am skilled in using Krita, Unity 2D AND 3D, and Aesprite :D!
